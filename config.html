About Todoist Integration

This extension integrates Mozilla Thunderbird with Todoist, allowing you to easily convert emails into actionable tasks.

Features

  • Convert emails to Todoist tasks with one click
  • Preserve email content and metadata
  • Choose target projects for better organization
  • Dark mode support
  • Secure token storage
